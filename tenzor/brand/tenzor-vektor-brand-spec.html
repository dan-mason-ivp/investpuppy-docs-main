01 Scope & Context
CompanyInvestPuppy Pte Ltd — Singapore-based fintech. Registered company identity uses the InvestPuppy name and logo (existing asset, not in scope here).
Products in scopeVektor — systematic listed equity portfolio management platform, boutique scale.
Tenzor — full portfolio management system, institutional scale. Second product, new mark required.
Brand tierBoth marks are Tier 1 — full dark treatment on dark background. Same visual register. Distinct identities connected by shared accent colour (Gold #C8A96E) and common maker's mark.
Maker's markBY INVESTPUPPY — the maker's mark appearing below both product wordmarks. Consistent treatment across all products. See Section 6.
What is NOT in scopeInvestPuppy company logo (existing, locked). Unvarnished sub-brand (existing, locked). Website design. Document templates.
Primary deliverable: The agency is commissioned to produce production-ready vector and raster assets for the Tenzor mark in all specified variants, and to update the Vektor mark (existing PNG asset provided) to align the BY INVESTPUPPY maker's mark treatment to the Treatment 4b specification below. All assets must be delivered in formats specified in Section 9.

06 Maker's Mark — Treatment 4b Specification
Treatment 4b — LOCKED May 2026. Confirmed by 16-member panel review and daylight mobile test. The maker's mark is consistent across all InvestPuppy products. Future products inherit this specification unchanged.
Architecture

Two-line colophon structure. BY appears above on a separate line, acting as a category label. INVESTPUPPY appears below, with INVEST in grey and PUPPY in verdigris — echoing the InvestPuppy company logo colour split (Invest white / Puppy green).

Precise specification

BY Poppins Regular / 400 70% of INVESTPUPPY size 0.30em #AAAAAA Centred

Sizing relationship to wordmark
Reference sizeThe INVESTPUPPY line size (reference size) should be approximately 9.5% of the wordmark cap-height at any given scale. Example: at a 100px wordmark cap-height, INVESTPUPPY is ~9.5px. BY is ~6.65px (70% of 9.5px).
Vertical gap (BY to INVESTPUPPY)Line-height 1.2 on INVESTPUPPY line. Gap between BY baseline and INVESTPUPPY cap-height: 2px at 8px reference size (proportional at other sizes).
Vertical gap (wordmark to BY)Spacing above the maker's mark block: approximately 18% of the wordmark fontSize. This creates visual connection without crowding.
Minimum maker's mark sizeINVESTPUPPY line no smaller than 5.5pt / 7.3px in any application. Below 14px (rendered wordmark size), apply Treatment 6 (single word, full #6BAF82) as mandatory variant — see Section 7.
Minimum-size variant (Treatment 6)
When the full mark is rendered below 14px wordmark size, the INVEST/PUPPY split is not reliably legible in adverse conditions (confirmed by daylight mobile test). Use Treatment 6 as the mandatory variant: BY (grey, small, above) / INVESTPUPPY (single word, Verdigris #6BAF82, below). All other specifications (typeface, weight, tracking, alignment) are identical to Treatment 4b.

09 Clear Space & Minimum Size
Clear space rule Minimum clear space on all four sides of the complete mark (wordmark + maker's mark) equals the cap-height of the first letter of the wordmark at the rendered size. This applies equally to Vektor and Tenzor, and to all three variants.
Minimum digital size Tenzor wordmark fontSize no smaller than 7px. Vektor PNG no smaller than 80px wide display width. Below these thresholds legibility cannot be guaranteed.
Minimum print size Wordmark cap-height no smaller than 4mm in any print application. Maker's mark INVESTPUPPY line no smaller than 5.5pt.
Clear space elements No other typographic elements, imagery, or graphic devices within the clear space zone. Background colour changes (e.g. a panel edge) may occur at the clear space boundary.

11 Prohibited Uses
Do not alter the letterform proportions or spacing of either wordmark.
Do not use the product marks on a non-dark background without explicit approval. Both marks are designed for dark (#0A0A0A) backgrounds. Monochrome variants exist for exceptions.
Do not use the InvestPuppy Green (#85D155) in either product wordmark or maker's mark. Green is the company identity colour only.
Do not apply a drop shadow, glow, gradient, or any other effect to either wordmark.
Do not rotate, skew, or distort either wordmark.
Do not place either mark at a size below the minimum legible threshold (7px digital / 4mm print cap-height).
Do not use PUPPY in any colour other than Verdigris (#6BAF82) in Treatment 4b, or White (#FFFFFF) in Variant C.
Do not render Variant A (with crossbar) below 18px. Use Variant B.
Do not use the Tenzor mark without the maker's mark in any public-facing context.
Do not modify the Vektor wordmark letterforms. The V arrowhead in Gold (#C8A96E) is an integral part of the locked asset.
