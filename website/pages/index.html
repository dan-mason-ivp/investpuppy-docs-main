Five reasons

Vektor gives boutique DPMs access to the same systematic portfolio management infrastructure used by institutional asset managers — without the institutional headcount, terminal costs, or integration complexity. The methodology is documented across eleven research papers. Not a black box.
Listed equities across any exchange, any currency. Multiple concurrent mandates, each with its own benchmark, constraints, and reporting. SGX is the primary use case at launch. The architecture is market-agnostic from the first line of code.
