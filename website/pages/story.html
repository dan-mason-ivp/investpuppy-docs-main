The Story

The Problem We Decided to Solve

Boutique DPMs are managing institutional-scale complexity with non-institutional tools. This is not a technology gap. It is an access gap.

The systematic portfolio management infrastructure that institutional asset managers use as a matter of course has historically been inaccessible at boutique scale. The terminal costs are prohibitive. The integration complexity assumes a large technology team. The consumer robo-tools automate away the judgment that defines boutique wealth management. Vektor exists to close that gap.

Three marks. One purpose.

Vektor — the platform

Systematic listed equity portfolio management. Built from first principles. Documented in full.

InvestPuppy — the company

We chose a name you would remember before we said another word about the product. That was the point. Serious when it matters.
