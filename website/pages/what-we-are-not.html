Scope and limitations

What Vektor Is Not

An honest description of scope, limitation, and design intent. We believe you should know what this platform does not do before we tell you what it does.

Not this

Not a black box. Every signal, model weight, and allocation decision is documented and auditable. You see exactly what the system is doing and why.

Not this

Not a substitute for investment judgment. Vektor supports the portfolio manager. It does not replace mandate interpretation, client relationship management, or regulatory judgment.

Not this

Not a guarantee of any outcome. Systematic methodology is not a promise of returns. Past performance does not guarantee future results.

Not this

Not an AI trading system. Vektor uses machine learning for specific, defined tasks. It is not an autonomous trading agent.

Not this · yet

Not live with a trading record. Vektor is currently in proof-of-concept stage under IBKR simulation. The Founding Mandate programme is how we build that record together.

Not this · yet

Not multi-asset. Vektor covers listed equities. Fixed income, alternatives, and private markets are on the roadmap. Not at launch.

Not this

Not a live track record. No. We are telling you this before you ask because it is the right thing to do. See WP-09 and WP-10.

Not this

Not a regulated product. InvestPuppy and Vektor do not hold investment management licences. Regulatory responsibility remains with the licensed entity using it.

Not this

Not for every mandate type. Vektor is built for discretionary listed equity mandates. It is the wrong tool for mandates requiring judgment-based allocation across asset classes.

Having been precise about what Vektor is not, here is what it is. With the same precision.

This

Systematic listed equity management — signal-driven, fully auditable, across any exchange and currency.

This

Multi-mandate — multiple concurrent mandates, each with its own benchmark, constraints, and reporting.

This

Institutional rigour — the same systematic infrastructure institutional asset managers use, accessible at boutique scale.

This

Human-gated — every allocation is reviewed before execution. The portfolio manager retains full authority.

This

Fully auditable — complete decision trail from signal to execution. Every step documented.

This

Proof-of-concept offer — Show us a mandate. We run the full Vektor workflow on your data. No slides. No promises.
